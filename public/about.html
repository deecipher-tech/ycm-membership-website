About Youth Concessive Movement

Discover our journey, mission, and commitment to empowering Nigerian youth for national development and positive change.

Our Story

The Youth Concessive Movement (YCM) was founded in 2015 by a group of young Nigerian professionals who recognized the urgent need for organized youth participation in national development. What began as a small advocacy group in Lagos has grown into a nationwide movement with members across all 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory.

Our founders, drawn from diverse professional backgrounds including education, technology, healthcare, and entrepreneurship, shared a common vision: to create a platform where young Nigerians could channel their energy, creativity, and passion toward building a better nation.

Over the years, YCM has evolved from conducting small community projects to implementing large-scale national initiatives. We've organized over 200 events, trained more than 5,000 young Nigerians in various skills, and established partnerships with government agencies, private sector organizations, and international development partners.

Today, YCM stands as one of Nigeria's most vibrant youth organizations, recognized for our commitment to civic engagement, leadership development, and community empowerment.

Our Core Principles

Guiding our actions and decisions toward youth empowerment

Our Mission

To empower Nigerian youth through comprehensive civic education, leadership development programs, and meaningful community engagement, creating a generation of proactive, informed, and responsible citizens committed to sustainable national development.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every young person has equal access to opportunities, possesses the necessary skills to thrive in the 21st century, and actively participates in shaping a prosperous, peaceful, and progressive nation that realizes its full potential.

Our Motto

"Empowered Youth, Progressive Nation"

We believe that the development of Nigeria is intrinsically linked to the empowerment of its youth. An empowered youth population creates a foundation for national progress, innovation, and sustainable development.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every aspect of our work

I

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our activities, ensuring transparency, accountability, and honesty in our operations and relationships.

E

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, continuously improving our programs and services to deliver maximum value to our members and communities.

I

Inclusivity

We welcome and value diversity, ensuring equal opportunities for all Nigerian youth regardless of gender, ethnicity, religion, or socioeconomic background.

I

Innovation

We embrace creative thinking and innovative solutions to address the complex challenges facing Nigerian youth and our nation as a whole.

C

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnership and actively collaborate with stakeholders across sectors to amplify our impact and reach.

P

Patriotism

We foster love for Nigeria and commitment to national development, encouraging members to contribute positively to their communities and country.
