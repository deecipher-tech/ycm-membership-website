Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about YCM

To become a YCM member, visit our Membership page and complete the registration form. Membership is open to Nigerian youth aged 16-35. You'll need to:

  • Provide basic personal information
  • Upload a passport photo
  • Provide valid identification
  • Complete the online orientation

Once submitted, your application will be reviewed and you'll receive a confirmation email within 3-5 business days.

No, basic membership with Youth Concessive Movement is completely free. We believe in making youth empowerment accessible to all young Nigerians.

However, certain special events, training programs, or certification courses may have minimal fees to cover materials, logistics, and expert facilitation. These fees are always clearly communicated in advance, and we offer scholarships and discounts for members in need.

YCM members receive priority registration for all events. Here's how to participate:

  1. Become a YCM member (if not already)
  2. Browse upcoming events on our Events page
  3. Register directly through the event page
  4. Receive confirmation and event details via email

Some events may have limited capacity, so early registration is recommended. Non-members can also attend many of our events, though members enjoy benefits like free admission, early registration, and exclusive access.

Yes! We actively encourage passionate members to start YCM chapters in their communities. Here's what you need:

  • Minimum of 10 interested youth members
  • A suitable meeting space (community center, school, etc.)
  • Commitment to organizing regular activities
  • Completion of chapter leadership training

Contact our Membership Director at chapters@ycm.ng to discuss the requirements and process. We provide comprehensive training, ongoing support, and resources for chapter leaders.
