Eligibility Requirements

Who Can Join?

Age Requirement

Must be between 16-35 years old

Nationality

Must be a Nigerian citizen

Residency

Must reside in Nigeria or be willing to participate in Nigerian activities

Commitment

Willing to abide by YCM constitution and values

Documents

Valid means of identification required

Required Documents

Prepare These Documents

Passport Photograph

Recent passport photo on white background (JPEG/PNG, max 2.5MB)

Voter's Card (Front)

Clear photo of front side of PVC (JPEG/PNG, max 2.5MB)

Voter's Card (Back)

Clear photo of back side of PVC (JPEG/PNG, max 2.5MB)

Important Notes:

  • • All documents must be clear and legible
  • • File size must not exceed 2.5MB per document
  • • Only JPEG and PNG formats are accepted
  • • Ensure all information is visible and not blurred
